Common Questions

Here are the answers to the questions we hear most often from our community members.

Notes are saved automatically to your private user storage in our Firestore database as soon as you hit the "Save Note" button. They are tied to your unique user ID and are synchronized in real-time across any device where you are logged in.

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. These intervals are known as "pomodoros." It encourages focused work and prevents burnout.
